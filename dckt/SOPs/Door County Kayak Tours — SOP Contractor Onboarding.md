## Pre-Hire Requirements

**Role:** Kayak Guide

- [ ] CPR/First Aid certification (current)
- [ ] Kayak or paddle sports instructor cert preferred (ACA, BCU, or equivalent)
- [ ] Swiftwater or open water rescue training (if applicable to tours)
- [ ] Background check completed
- [ ] Swim proficiency confirmed

## Documents to Collect Before First Shift

Guides are W-2 employees, paid through Homebase. Collect the following:

- [ ] **W-4** — Federal tax withholding (employee, not contractor)
- [ ] **I-9** — Employment eligibility verification
- [ ] **Signed Employment Agreement** — via DocuSeal (e-sign)
- [ ] **Photo ID** — Driver's license or passport (required for I-9)
- [ ] Copy of CPR/First Aid certification
- [ ] Direct deposit info — set up in Homebase
- [ ] Emergency contact information

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Offer & Agreement (Before Anything Else)
- Send DocuSeal employment agreement link via email
- Confirm they've signed before scheduling any shifts
- Collect W-4 and I-9 — upload to Homebase

### Step 2: Orientation Day (In-Person)
- Walk through the Pre-Tour Preparation SOP with new guide
- Equipment tour: where everything lives, how to inspect, how to store
- Emergency procedures: who to call, where the first aid kit is, incident report process
- Safety briefing practice — new hire delivers it, manager gives feedback
- Guest communication expectations: tone, response time, how to handle complaints

### Step 3: Shadow Shift (Shift 1)
- New guide observes experienced guide for full tour
- No independent guest interaction — observation only
- Debrief after: questions, feedback, go/no-go for next step

### Step 4: Assisted Shift (Shifts 2-3)
- New guide leads with experienced guide present
- Experienced guide steps in only if safety concern or guest issue
- Manager reviews post-shift feedback

### Step 5: Solo Sign-Off
- Manager confirms: guest handling, safety procedures, gear competency
- Sign-off documented below (add date and manager name)
- Guide added to scheduling rotation in Homebase

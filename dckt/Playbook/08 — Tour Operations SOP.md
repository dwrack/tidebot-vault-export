## Tour Capacity & Pricing

- **Max group size:** 14 guests per guide
- **Over 14 (single party only):** 2 guides required + both vans
- **Returning customer discount:** 20% off per-tour
- **Private tours:** Available — check FareHarbor for current pricing
- **Personal kayaks:** Allowed if SOT with bulkheads, guide-approved. $10 discount per boat.
- **Personal PFDs:** Allowed if Type 3 PFD

---

## Booking Policy

- **24-hour cancellation policy**
- If party cancels < 24 hrs:
  - If we fill all spots → no charge (keep what they paid)
  - If we can't fill all spots → charge for vacant spots + mail gift certificates for free tours
- **Never use the word "refund"** — use "reschedule" or "gift certificate"
- **Weather cancellations:** If conditions are unsafe, offer reschedule or tour type change

---

## Scheduling Best Practice

- **Push 8am tours** — fills morning, leaves afternoon open for same-day walk-ins
- Suggest early in the vacation week so there's room to reschedule if needed
- Optimal party size per slot: ~12 (max van capacity = 14)

## Phone Script

**Greeting:** "Door County Kayak and Bike Tours, this is _____."

**Key questions to ask:**
- Which day and time? → Book in FareHarbor
- How many in party?
- Any children? Ages?
- Best phone number while in Door County?
- Have you kayaked before? What's your experience level?
- Do you have our updated directions to the Jacksonport location?

**Tour info to convey:**
- Tours are weather-dependent — guide decides location ~20 min before departure
- Never guarantee Cave Point in advance
- Tours are ~3 hours total
- Arrive 15–20 minutes before scheduled start
- What to wear: layers, synthetic fabrics, shoes that can get wet (no jeans/cotton)
- Bring: water, sunscreen, hat, dry change of clothes

**Never talk about:**
- Previous drunken stories
- Alcohol preferences
- Profanity, sexual topics, or inappropriate jokes

## FAQ (Reception Reference)

**"We only want Cave Point — can we cancel if we don't go there?"**
Sign up for a different tour/rental or different time if the weather is appropriate. If no availability, we allow cancellation.

**"Can I bring my own kayak? Is there a discount?"**
Yes if SOT with bulkheads and guide-approved. $10 discount per boat.

**"Can we bring our dog?"**
Dependent on weather and dog. Dog must have a lifejacket.

**"My wife is pregnant — can she come?"**
She should consult her personal physician. We've had pregnant women on tour. Only a doctor can make that call.

**"How likely am I to tip over?"**
Rare. Stable sit-on-top tandems. Guide is trained in water rescue. We've never had a safety issue with non-swimmers.

**"Is there somewhere to stop for a bathroom?"**
Every tour is different. Good idea to use bathroom before we leave. On-water options vary.

**"Is tipping appropriate? How much?"**
Guide tips are very much appreciated. We suggest 18%. Space on receipt.

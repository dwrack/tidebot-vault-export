## Permit 1: Garrett Bay Commercial Launch

**What it covers:** Commercial kayak launch at 1310 Garrett Bay Road, Ellison Bay, WI — Door Bluff / Shipwreck Tour

**Issuing authority:** Town of Liberty Grove
**Contact:** Pamela Donart-Welcome, Clerk/Treasurer *(Anastasia Bell previously held this role through ~2023)*
**Email:** town@libertygrovewi.gov
**Address:** 11161 Old Stage Road, Sister Bay, WI 54234

**Cost:** ~$1,200/year (covers 2 trailer loads = 24 boats)
**Process:**
1. Contact Town Clerk in early spring (March)
2. Complete Commercial Launch Permit Application (PDF — on file)
3. Return application + fee to Town Hall
4. Confirm COI from Zodiac Insurance is attached

**Key constraint:** Permit covers up to 2 trailer loads of boats (max ~24 kayaks). Do not exceed this.

**History:** Permit has been renewed annually. Paul Haan (former GM) submitted in 2023. Relationship with Town Clerk is positive.

## Permit 2: Cave Point County Park

**What it covers:** Commercial launch at 5648 Schauer Rd, Jacksonport — Cave Point sea caves tour

**Launch details:**
- Off Hwy 57 → Cave Point Drive → Schauer Rd
- Park on gravel. Kayak entrance on sandy side of pier.
- Water is shallow — may need to walk out to avoid scraping
- GPS: Sturgeon Bay, WI 54235 (GPS says Sturgeon Bay — confirm with guests)

**Permit status:** ⚠️ Needs annual confirmation — contact Door County Parks or Whitefish Dunes State Park

**Notes:**
- Cave Point has picnic tables, grills, and public restrooms
- NEVER guarantee Cave Point in advance — weather-dependent

## Permit 3: Eco Estuary (Rieboldt Creek)

**Address:** Rieboldt Creek off County Q, Baileys Harbor → Murphy Park

**Launch details:**
- North from Baileys Harbor → right on County Q → ~3-4 miles → gravel road on right before bridge over Mud River
- Park at Murphy Park (minimal parking — do not block access)
- Do not pull car past sign limiting access to estuary
- Follow dark/deeper channel if water is low

## Memorial Day Parade — Special Operations

The Town of Jacksonport holds MaiFest every Memorial Day weekend:
- Parade + festival Saturday
- Run/walk all day Sunday
- **Hwy 57 is closed 9:30am–12pm Saturday**

**Tour logistics when parade is running:**
1. Collect guests at Jacksonport Historical Society Grounds
2. Take Jorns Rd → Schauer Park to reach Cave Point launch
3. Keep one receptionist at shop for logistics
4. Second person at tour staging site for receipts/check-ins
5. Use e-bikes to move between shop and staging site if needed
6. Bring phone cases + waters for purchase at staging site
7. Porta-potties at launch available for guests

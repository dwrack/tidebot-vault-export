# ACA Guide Certification

**Organization:** American Canoe Association (ACA)
**Standard:** Paddling safety and technical skill development

---

## What It Is

The ACA certifies kayak guides through workshops conducted by an ACA Instructor. Certification confirms guides meet national standards for paddling safety.

## Why It Matters for DCKT

- Liability protection — certified guides reduce insurance exposure
- Guest trust — verifiable credential
- Quality control — ensures consistent guide skill level
- May be required by some group/corporate clients

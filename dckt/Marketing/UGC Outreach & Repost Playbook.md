# UGC Outreach & Repost Playbook — DCKT + Door County Ebike

**Owner:** Bella (business manager) / Lea (social/comms)
**Frequency:** Weekly (15-20 min search + outreach as needed)

---

## Why This Matters

Door County is a destination people photograph obsessively. The caves, the cliffs, the water, the fall colors, the small-town charm. Kayakers and ebikers are already creating gorgeous content. We need to find it, get permission, and put it on our profiles. This covers both kayak tours and ebike tours since they share a brand presence.

> Hey just circling back on this, totally no pressure either way!

## Story Recovery Outreach

Stories disappear after 24 hours but the person still has the video on their phone. When you see a great Story tagging or mentioning DCKT or the ebike tours, DM them right away and ask them to repost as a Reel or feed post so we can share it. We'll tag them in the repost and in any ad we run with it.

**Check story mentions/tags daily (2 min). Don't wait for the weekly search.**

### When You See a Great Story

> Hey [name]! Just saw your Story from your [kayak tour / ebike ride] with us and it was beautiful. Any chance you'd post it as a Reel or regular post? We'd love to share it on our page and tag you. We sometimes feature our favorite customer videos in ads too, and we'd tag you there as well. Totally up to you!

### If They Ask What They Get

> We'll tag you in everything: the repost on our feed and any ad we run with it. Free exposure to our whole audience. And honestly we just think your video was great and want more people to see it.

### If You Missed the Story (even weeks later)

> Hey [name]! You posted a Story from your [kayak tour / ebike ride] in Door County a little while back and it was stunning. Any chance you still have the video and would be down to post it as a Reel? We'd love to share it and tag you, and we sometimes run our favorites as ads where you'd be tagged too.

## Repost Format

### Caption Template

> [Short caption matching the vibe]
>
> Thanks to @[creator_handle] for sharing their [day on the water / ride through Door County]!
>
> #doorcountykayak #doorcounty #doorcountywi #cavepointkayak #wisconsin

**Rules:**
- Always tag the creator
- No sales pitch
- 2-3 lines max before the credit tag
- 3-5 hashtags
- Use kayak or ebike hashtags depending on the content

### Download & Save
- Use snapinsta.app (IG) or fdown.net (FB) to download
- Save to `Assets/Videos/UGC/` with filename: `2026-04-16_creator-handle_description.mp4`

### Posting Cadence
1-2 UGC reposts per week during season. Cave/cliff footage and golden hour water shots tend to perform best.

---

## Flag Top Performers for Ads

After a repost has been live 5-7 days, check performance. Flag it if:
- Views are 2x+ above your average
- Notable saves
- Comments asking about booking, pricing, or "where is this?"
- Shares significantly above average

**How to flag:** Message David in Slack with the post link, quick stats, and "recommend for ad." David handles all ad creation from UGC.

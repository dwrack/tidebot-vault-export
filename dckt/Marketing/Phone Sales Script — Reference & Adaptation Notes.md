# Phone Sales Script — Reference & Adaptation Notes

> **Source context:** This script was originally written for Mad Moose (a UTV/off-road adventure company in Southern Utah). It contains a full inbound phone sales framework that is directly adaptable for DCKT. The CCLOSER framework and call structure are high-quality and worth implementing.

> The document also contains chatbot feedback notes at the top — those appear to be notes about the NOLA Party Barge chatbot (boat names, pricing, flow issues). These are NOLA-specific and not relevant to DCKT.

---

## Call Standards (Non-Negotiable)
- Target call length: ~10 minutes
- Ask for the sale on every call
- Price range can be shared before full discovery: give a range, not final price
- Final price never before discovery
- Minimum booking value: establish DCKT equivalent

---

## CCLOSER Framework (Alex Hormozi / Acquisition.com)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **C** — Clarify | How did you hear about us? Why this trip? Other plans? |
| **C** — Compliment | "Glad you found us." / "Sounds like a fantastic trip." |
| **L** — Label | Acknowledge the challenge of planning the right experience |
| **O** — Overview | Most people call several companies before choosing |
| **S** — Sell the Outcome | Unforgettable scenery, expert guides, best kayaking in the Midwest |
| **E** — Explain | Best experience guarantee, safety-first approach, flexible scheduling |
| **R** — Reinforce | Resell confidence after the decision is made |

## Call Structure

### 1. Case Open (First 30–60 Seconds)
**Rapport building:**
> "Thanks for calling Door County Kayak Tours! My name is [Name]. Who am I speaking with today?"

**Proof points to build excitement** (use 2–4, not all):
- "We're one of the top-rated kayak tour companies on the peninsula"
- "Guests tell us it's the highlight of their Door County trip"
- "Our guides are ACA-certified and know every inch of the coastline"
- "We do everything from beginner-friendly sunset paddles to full-day sea cave tours"
- "We keep groups small so every guest gets real attention"
- "Easy online booking — or I can get you set up right now"

**Permission to move on:**
> "Great! Let me get that pulled up so I can take care of you."

---

### 2. Call Control & Position
> "I'm going to ask you a couple of questions to make sure you have an incredible experience."

---

### 3. Needs Audit — Discovery Questions
- "Have you kayaked with us before, or is this your first time in Door County?"
- "How long will you be in the area?"
- "How many people are coming, and who's in the group?"
- "Any first-time paddlers or kids?"
- "What are you looking for — scenic and relaxed, or a bit more adventurous?"
- "Any special occasion — birthday, anniversary, bachelorette?"
- "How did you hear about us?"

**Listen actively:**
> "So you're looking for a relaxed sunset paddle for a group of six — is that right?"

**Qualify & compliment:**
> "Great job planning ahead — Door County summers book up fast."

---

### 4. Solution Presentation
> "Based on what you mentioned, I'd recommend our [Sunset Tour / Cave Point Tour / Private Charter] — it's perfect for [their stated need]."

**Use proof:**
> "Guests always tell us this is the highlight of their whole Door County trip."

---

### 5. Handling Objections

| Concern | Response |
|---------|----------|
| Price | "I totally understand — our tours include everything: guides, equipment, safety gear. Most guests say it's the best value of their trip." |
| Never kayaked | "We take first-timers out every day. Our guides do a full on-water orientation before we paddle anywhere." |
| Weather concern | "We monitor conditions daily. If we need to reschedule, we'll get you moved to a date that works." |
| Group size | "We can accommodate [X] — let me check availability right now." |
| Kids | "We take kids as young as [age] — they're often the best paddlers out there!" |

---

### 6. Closing
> "Let me confirm — [date/time], [tour type], [number of guests]."

> "This is going to be incredible. Door County looks completely different from the water."

**Next steps:** Confirmation email, waiver link, what to bring, where to park.

**If not booking:**
> "No worries — glad you found us. If plans change, we'd love to take care of you."

---

### 7. Upsells at Fulfillment
- Private charter upgrade
- Extended tour option
- Gear rental add-ons
- Gift cards

---

## Operating Rule
> Diagnose first. Prescribe confidently. Ask for the sale. Every call.

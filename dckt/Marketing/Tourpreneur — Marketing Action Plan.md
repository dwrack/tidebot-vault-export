## 🟠 This Month

### Experience Design
- [ ] **Map the emotional arc** of each DCKT tour on paper — identify where the peak is; if unclear, design one (VL06, GROW02)
- [ ] **Add a narrative priming segment** to the pre-launch briefing: "By the end of today, you'll understand something about this shoreline that most people never see" (VL05)
- [ ] **Define one "narrative withholding" sequence** — plant an anticipation seed 10-20 minutes before the peak reveal (GT01, VL05)
- [ ] **Add a 60-second structured reflection** at the end of the tour — one question as the kayaks return to dock (VL05)
- [ ] **Guide training: no neutral time** — every transit and waiting moment assigned a purpose (story, question, observation task) (VL06)

### Copywriting & Website
- [ ] **Audit all tour descriptions**: trim intro to <200 words, ensure exactly 6 highlights per tour (GC04)
- [ ] **Rewrite the About page** to show real guide personalities, photos, and their Door County story (GC04)
- [ ] **Name the food** on any tours that include snacks/refreshments — use hedging language: "you might try..." (GC04)
- [ ] **Scan all copy for AI cliché adjectives** ("captivating," "vibrant," "bustling") — replace with specific concrete details (GC04)
- [ ] **Rewrite booking confirmation email** with one values-demonstrating story: who are the guides, why do they do this? (GC35)
- [ ] **Create a one-page tone-of-voice document**: brand personality adjectives, example paragraph, consistent language rules (GC04)

### Content & SEO
- [ ] **Write one Dreaming-stage blog post**: "Planning Your Door County Trip — What to Do Beyond the Wineries" — positions DCKT as local expert, captures planning-stage visitors (TMA-01)
- [ ] **Write one evergreen SEO blog post**: "Everything You Need to Know About Kayaking Door County's Sea Caves" (DEMAND, CON-S02)
- [ ] **Create a lead magnet PDF**: "The Complete Sea Cave Kayaking Guide — Door County" — add to website + Instagram bio (GGA-01, DEMAND)
- [ ] **Film one face-to-camera intro video**: guide at the dock, 60 seconds, natural light — post to TikTok + Reels + YouTube Shorts (LIVE16, GGA-01)
- [ ] **Sign up for TubeBuddy** (free trial) — find top 3 keyword opportunities for Door County kayak content (GGA-01)

### Technical SEO
- [ ] **AltText.ai**: check WordPress media library image count → purchase credits → install plugin and run bulk generation with keywords: "Door County kayak tours," "sea caves kayak," "Great Lakes kayaking," "private tours" (HACK02)

## 🟡 Next 90 Days

### Brand
- [ ] **Write brand adjectives** (5): e.g., wild, expert, quiet, local, real — audit current website and social against them (VL01)
- [ ] **Create a mood board** on Pinterest (20+ images): Yeti, REI, National Geographic Traveler, sea caves photography (GC12)
- [ ] **Commission a half-day golden-hour photography session** at the sea caves — this asset library serves 2-3 years (GC12)
- [ ] **Write a positioning statement**: "The tour for people who want Door County the way the locals know it — not the way TripAdvisor does" (VL01)

### Marketing Infrastructure
- [ ] **Install Facebook pixel and Google Analytics** with conversion tracking on all booking pages (CON-S02)
- [ ] **Set up Facebook retargeting audience** from website visitors — cheapest, highest-ROI ad spend available (CON-S02)
- [ ] **Build a cancellation re-engagement email**: "Rebook and we'll include X" — upsell not discount (HACK04)
- [ ] **Post-tour anniversary email**: one year after booking, send a guest photo + rebooking invitation (HACK04)

### Media & PR
- [ ] **Identify 3 outdoor/adventure journalists** (Outside, Midwest Living, REI Journal) — read their most recent article; draft a personalized outreach (GC07)
- [ ] **Reach out to regional press first**: Door County Pulse, Isthmus (Madison), Milwaukee Journal Sentinel travel section (GC07)
- [ ] **Trend attachment pitch**: "Door County's sea caves are becoming a bucket-list kayak destination — we've been running these tours for X years" (GC07)

### Community & Impact
- [ ] **Identify one Door County conservation partner** (shoreline preservation, land trust) — add "$X per booking goes to…" to website narrative (GC24)
- [ ] **Research Potawatomi or regional Indigenous storytelling partnerships** for a premium cultural kayak experience (GC30)
- [ ] **Add one "community story" to the website**: who are the real humans behind DCKT, and why do they do it? (GC30)

### Premium & Distribution
- [ ] **Design one private/exclusive product** priced with 15% agent commission margin built in (GC34)
- [ ] **Create a beautiful pre-trip briefing PDF** for private/premium bookings — guide story, what to expect, Door County lore (GC34)
- [ ] **Identify 2-3 local luxury hotel concierges** in Door County — introduce yourself and the private tour product (GC34)
- [ ] **Reframe sunrise/early morning paddle as premium**: "before the crowds, when the water is glass" — higher price, not lower (GROW02)

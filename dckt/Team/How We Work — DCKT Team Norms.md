# How We Work — DCKT Team Norms
*David & Bella | 2026 Season*

---

## Communication

- **Obsidian vault** — source of truth. Decisions, plans, and processes live here.
- **Text** — quick questions, urgent ops issues
- **Weekly check-in** — Bella fills out every Friday (see [[Team/Bella/Weekly Check-In Template]])
- **Standing call** — TBD cadence after onboarding (suggest bi-weekly 30 min)
- **Rule:** if something is decided over text or phone, one of us logs it in the vault

---

## Task Ownership

- [[Team/Open Items]] is the shared to-do list — both of us check it daily
- If you pick something up, add your name and move it to "In Progress"
- If you're blocked, flag it in Open Items under Blockers — don't sit on it
- Done means done — mark it complete and note any follow-up needed

---

## Decision Making

- Bella owns: HR system setup, onboarding process, guide contract language, hiring pipeline, housing deposit process
- David owns: final approval on systems/spend, vendor relationships, business direction
- When in doubt: propose in the vault, discuss on call, decide together

---

## Accountability

- Weekly check-in is non-negotiable — it keeps us aligned without requiring constant calls
- Open Items is a live document — stale tasks get a conversation, not a pass
- Bella's first 30 days: focus on HR system + onboarding + hiring. Nothing else until that's running.

---

## Vault Etiquette

- Use the existing folder structure — don't create new top-level folders without checking
- Link related notes using `[[note name]]` so everything stays connected
- Date your notes when relevant — makes it easy to track what changed and when
- Don't delete — archive instead (move to an `Archive/` subfolder)

# Houston Pedal Barge — SOP: Pay & Labor Costs
*Last updated: 2026-04-07*

> **Purpose:** Establish clear pay rates across all roles and markets so we can track labor costs accurately and avoid ad hoc compensation decisions.

---

## Pedal Barge Guides (Austin & Houston)

Pay is per-trip and scales with group size. There is no hourly component.

| Group Size | Pay Per Trip |
|---|---|
| 2-3 guests (small tour) | $40 |
| Up to 8 guests (regular group) | $60 |
| 9+ guests (guide alone, solo-handling) | $80 |

**Note:** The $50/3-guest rate previously used (Reid) has been discontinued. $40 is the floor for small tours.

---

## Pedal Barge Captains — Austin (Established)

Bella and Wreck have their own rates reflecting tenure and role.

| Condition | Bella | Wreck |
|---|---|---|

This covers run lights, power washing, deep cleaning bathrooms, drive shaft maintenance, and similar work. These hours are tracked and paid separately from trip pay at rates to be confirmed.

|---|---|

## Rental Staffing Logic

Rentals require on-site coverage but we don't staff speculatively. Current approach:

- **Mon-Wed (and sometimes Thurs):** No staff stationed if no reservations are on the books.
- **Busy or uncertain days:** Owner makes judgment calls on when to put someone on site vs. risk the no-show cost.
- **Slow days:** Staff is sent home after 3 hours minimum rather than keeping them on a dead shift.

The alternative to this model is salaried coverage, which isn't viable right now given current revenue. Until volume increases, the educated-gamble staffing model stays in place.

---

## Ad Hoc / Project Labor

When contract workers are brought in for one-off projects (construction, launch site work, etc.), pay is negotiated separately. For reference, the most recent example was $15/hour for a full day (9 AM to 6:30 PM) for two workers brought in through a personal contact to cover a staffing gap during the post-Reid transition period.

Document any ad hoc labor separately with dates, hours, and project description for accounting purposes.

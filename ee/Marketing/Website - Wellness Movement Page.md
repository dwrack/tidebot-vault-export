## About The Wellness Movement For All

We partnered with Willie McBride and The Wellness Movement For All to bring guided movement programming to the dock at Ebb and Ember.

Willie is a Portland-based coach, trainer, and adventurer with over 14 years of experience helping people move. He studied training and coaching at the National Personal Training Institute in Portland and has worked with hundreds of clients across a huge range of goals: foundational functional fitness, lifestyle changes, backpacking, mountaineering, road marathons, triathlons, and ultra marathons from 50k to 200+ miles.

But the thing that drives Willie most is helping people figure out what big goal actually moves them. A lot of people have the drive and the passion but don't know where to point it or how to start. That's where he comes in.

## The Sessions

### Holistic Fitness Series

A 3-week guided fitness series on the dock, followed by a full sauna session.

- **When:** Weekday mornings, 6:30 - 9:00 AM
- **Format:** 6:30 - 7:30 AM guided movement on the dock, 7:30 - 9:00 AM sauna session. If you need to head out after the movement portion, that's totally fine.
- **Led by:** Willie McBride / The Wellness Movement For All
- **Group size:** Max 8 people
- **Price:** $185 per person for the full 3-week series
- **What to bring:** Comfortable clothes you can move in, a water bottle, and a towel for the sauna. We have everything else.

## What to Expect

You show up to the dock at 6:30 AM. Willie (or Linnea) leads you through a movement or meditation session right on the water, with the Columbia in front of you and Mt. Hood behind. After that, you walk straight onto the sauna boat for a 90-minute session: heat, cold plunge, and quiet.

It's fitness and recovery in one morning. No gym. No rush. Just the dock, the river, and the sauna.

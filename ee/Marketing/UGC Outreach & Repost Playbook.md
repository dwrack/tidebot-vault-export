## Story Recovery Outreach

Stories disappear after 24 hours but the person still has the video on their phone. When you see a great Story tagging or mentioning Ebb & Ember, DM them right away and ask them to repost as a Reel or feed post so we can share it. We'll tag them in the repost and in any ad we run with it.

**Check story mentions/tags daily (2 min). Don't wait for the weekly search.** Sauna/cold plunge Stories are especially common because people want to show off the experience in the moment.

### When You See a Great Story

> Hey [name]! Just saw your Story from your session with us and it was incredible. Any chance you'd post it as a Reel or regular post? We'd love to share it on our page and tag you. We sometimes feature our favorite customer videos in ads too, and we'd tag you there as well. Totally up to you!

### If They Ask What They Get

> We'll tag you in everything: the repost on our feed and any ad we run with it. Free exposure to our whole audience. And honestly we just think your video was great and want more people to see it.

### If You Missed the Story (even weeks later)

> Hey [name]! You posted a Story from your time at Ebb & Ember a little while back and it looked amazing. Any chance you still have the video and would be down to post it as a Reel? We'd love to share it and tag you, and we sometimes run our favorites as ads where you'd be tagged too.

## Repost Format

### Caption Template

> [Short caption matching the vibe, lean into the transformation/feeling]
>
> Thanks to @[creator_handle] for sharing their experience with us!
>
> #ebbandember #portlandsauna #floatingsauna #coldplunge #portlandwellness

**Rules:**
- Always tag the creator
- No sales pitch. The steam and the river do the selling.
- 2-3 lines max before the credit tag
- 3-5 hashtags
- Wellness/transformation language fits this brand

### Download & Save
- Use snapinsta.app (IG) or fdown.net (FB) to download
- Save to `Assets/Videos/UGC/` with filename: `2026-04-16_creator-handle_description.mp4`

### Posting Cadence
1-2 UGC reposts per week. Steam/cold plunge contrast shots and reaction videos tend to perform best.

---

## Flag Top Performers for Ads

After a repost has been live 5-7 days, check performance. Flag it if:
- Views are 2x+ above your average
- Notable saves (huge in wellness space)
- Comments asking about booking, pricing, or "I need to try this"
- Shares significantly above average

**How to flag:** Message David in Slack with the post link, quick stats, and "recommend for ad." David handles all ad creation from UGC.

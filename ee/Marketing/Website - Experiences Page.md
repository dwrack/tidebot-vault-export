## About the Instructor

*Section below the cards. Text + image side by side.*

**Photo:** Willie mountain portrait (yellow jacket, Alps backdrop)

Willie McBride is a Portland-based coach, trainer, and adventurer. He's the founder of The Wellness Movement For All and has been coaching for over 14 years. He studied training and coaching at the National Personal Training Institute in Portland and has worked with hundreds of clients across a huge range of goals: foundational functional fitness, lifestyle changes, backpacking, mountaineering, road marathons, triathlons, and ultra marathons from 50k to 200+ miles.

The thing that drives Willie most is helping people figure out what big goal actually moves them. A lot of people have the passion but don't always know where to point it. That's where he comes in.

# SOP — Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Official policy:** No refunds for any reason.
**In practice:** Lenient — gift cards issued last-minute for illness, emergencies, etc.

---

## Steps

1. Customer requests cancellation
2. Do NOT issue a cash/card refund
3. Issue a gift card for the full amount paid
4. Send gift card to customer via email
5. Note the cancellation in Periode

## Edge Cases

| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| Customer is sick, same day | Issue gift card, no questions asked |
| Customer no-shows without contact | Gift card at your discretion |
| Weather concern (customer-initiated) | Gift card |
| Booking error on our end | Full refund |
| Double-booked by system error | Full refund + offer rebook |

---

## Script (text/email)

> "Hi [Name] — totally understand. We don't issue refunds per our policy, but we'll convert your booking to a gift card you can use any time. I'll send that over now. Hope to see you out on the water soon!"

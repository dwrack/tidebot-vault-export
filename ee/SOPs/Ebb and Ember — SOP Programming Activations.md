# Ebb and Ember — SOP: Programming Activations
*2026-03-31*

> **Purpose:** A consistent process for onboarding, approving, and launching any external programming — fitness series, meditation, events, workshops, pop-ups, or other activations. Every activation follows these steps. No exceptions.

---

## Stage 1: Intake & Concept Review

- [ ] Receive proposal from instructor/partner
- [ ] Review for fit: brand alignment, schedule gaps, audience, format
- [ ] Confirm availability of space and time slots
- [ ] Initial approval from David + Jonah before moving forward

---

## Stage 2: Insurance & Legal (Before Anything Is Confirmed)

All of the following must be in hand before any dates are locked or promotion begins.

**Lead Instructor / Partner:**
- [ ] Certificate of Liability Insurance received
- [ ] Commercial General Liability confirmed on cert
- [ ] Professional Liability confirmed on cert
- [ ] **Ebb & Ember Floating Saunas** named as Additional Insured
- [ ] **Elevated Tides, LLC** named as Additional Insured
- [ ] Both AI addresses correct: 10350 N. Vancouver Way #5411, Portland, OR 97217

**Subcontractors (if any):**
- [ ] Each subcontractor has their own cert OR
- [ ] Written confirmation from lead's insurer that subcontractors are covered under the lead's policy

**Written Agreement:**
- [ ] Signed agreement in place before first session (activates Additional Insured status on cert)
- [ ] Agreement covers: financial split, schedule, session format, cancellation terms, liability

> Note: A cert without a signed written agreement does not activate Additional Insured coverage. Both are required.

---

## Stage 3: Financial Agreement

- [ ] Revenue split agreed and documented in writing
- [ ] Payment terms confirmed (per session, per series, per participant, etc.)
- [ ] Instructor compensation confirmed
- [ ] E&E share confirmed
- [ ] Any platform/processing fees accounted for

---

## Stage 4: Walkthrough Session

Before any public-facing sessions take place:

- [ ] Schedule a private walkthrough session for David, Jonah, and E&E partners
- [ ] Weekdays only
- [ ] Instructor delivers the session as they would for the public
- [ ] E&E team evaluates: quality, tone, safety, brand fit, use of space
- [ ] Go / No-Go decision documented before promotion begins

|---|---|---|---|---|
| | | | | |

---

## Stage 5: Promotional Materials

- [ ] Written session description received from instructor (for website and social)
- [ ] Description reviewed and approved
- [ ] Passed to social media manager
- [ ] Dates, times, pricing confirmed for listing
- [ ] Booking/registration link set up (if applicable)

---

## Stage 6: Launch & Execution

- [ ] Dates locked on E&E calendar
- [ ] Promotion live (social, email, website)
- [ ] Instructor confirmed and briefed on day-of logistics
- [ ] Guest communication template prepared
- [ ] Post-series debrief scheduled

---

## Stage 7: Post-Activation Review

- [ ] Attendance and revenue reconciled
- [ ] Guest feedback collected
- [ ] Instructor debrief completed
- [ ] Decision: continue, adjust, or sunset
- [ ] Learnings documented for future activations

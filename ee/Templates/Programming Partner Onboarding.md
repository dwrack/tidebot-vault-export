# Welcome to Ebb & Ember — Programming Partner Onboarding

Thank you for your interest in bringing your practice to Ebb & Ember Floating Saunas. We're excited to build something special together. Below is everything you need to know to get started.

Please review this document carefully and reach out with any questions. We want the process to be smooth and the experience to be great — for you and for our guests.


## About the Space

Ebb & Ember is Portland's premier floating sauna experience, located on the water at the Elevated Tides Marina. Our guests come for restoration, connection, and something genuinely different. Programming that runs through E&E should reflect those same values — intentional, high quality, and well-suited to the unique environment.


## Before We Can Confirm Anything

The following items must be received and approved before any dates are locked, promotion begins, or sessions take place.

**1. Certificate of Liability Insurance**

You must carry active Professional Liability and Commercial General Liability insurance. Your certificate must name the following as Additional Insureds:

- Ebb & Ember Floating Saunas — 10350 N. Vancouver Way #5411, Portland, OR 97217
- Elevated Tides, LLC — 10350 N. Vancouver Way #5411, Portland, OR 97217

If you work with subcontractors or co-instructors, they must either carry their own policy or you must provide written confirmation from your insurer that they are covered under yours.

**2. Signed Programming Agreement**

A simple written agreement covering session format, schedule, financial terms, and liability. This also activates your Additional Insured status on the insurance certificate — both are required together.

**3. W-9**

Required for all independent contractors for tax purposes.

**4. Session Description**

A short written description of your session (2-5 sentences) for use on our website and in promotions. We may lightly edit for tone and consistency.


## The Walkthrough

Before your series opens to the public, we ask that you lead a private session for the Ebb & Ember team. This gives us a chance to experience your work firsthand, align on logistics, and make sure everything feels right in the space.

Walkthroughs are scheduled on weekdays. We'll coordinate a time that works for both sides.


## What We Provide

- Access to the dock and sauna space for the duration of your session
- Promotion through our social media, email list, and website
- Booking and guest communication support
- Towels and standard amenities for your participants


## What You're Responsible For

- Carrying and maintaining your own professional liability insurance
- Delivering sessions safely, professionally, and on time
- Communicating any cancellations to us as early as possible (minimum 48 hours notice)
- Screening participants for any contraindications relevant to your modality
- Knowing and following E&E's emergency procedures (reviewed at walkthrough)


## Cancellations & Minimums

- If you need to cancel a session, notify us at least 48 hours in advance
- We reserve the right to cancel a session if minimum participant threshold is not met (to be agreed upon in your programming agreement)
- Weather or site conditions may occasionally require rescheduling — we'll communicate this as early as possible


## Safety

Your participants will be using our sauna before, during, or after your session. Please familiarize yourself with sauna contraindications and be prepared to communicate them to guests. E&E staff will be on-site and available for any questions or emergencies. A full safety briefing is part of the walkthrough.


## Content & Photography

Any photos or video captured during your sessions at E&E may be used for promotional purposes by both you and Ebb & Ember, provided both parties are credited appropriately. Please ask before posting content that prominently features guests who have not given consent.

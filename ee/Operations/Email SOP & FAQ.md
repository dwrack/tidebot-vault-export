## STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

### 1. Cancellations & Rebooking

**Policy:** All sales are final. No refunds. Store credit via gift card only.

**Same-day / illness cancellations (customer emails in)**
1. Issue a gift card manually in Periode.no for the session value
2. Reply: *"Issued gift card. Check confirmation email > 'Booking Info' for future changes and gift card code."*
3. No exceptions needed — just do it without friction. Good will pays off.

**Customer cancels themselves in advance**
- System automatically issues store credit (promo code)
- If they email asking about it: *"Check your confirmation email > 'Booking Info' for your credit promo code."*

**Template reply (illness/same-day):**
> Issued gift card. Check your confirmation email under "Booking Info" for your gift card code — use it any time for a future booking.
> Ebb & Ember

---

### 2. Door Code Access Issues

**Root cause:** Door code only appears in the booking portal *after all waivers are signed.*

**If customer can't find their door code:**
1. Ask if all party members have signed their waivers
2. Direct them to the booking link in their confirmation email > "Booking Info"
3. If issue persists, text 503-308-1293

**Template reply:**
> Your door code appears in your booking once all required waivers are signed. Go to your confirmation email > "Booking Info" to view it. If you're still stuck, text us at 503-308-1293 and we'll sort it right away.

### 5. Partnership / Collaboration Inquiries

**Approach:** Start small, pilot first, expand if proven.

**Framework for replies:**
- Identify which time slots are underperforming (weekday early mornings are the priority gap)
- Propose a 3–5 week pilot, 1–2 sessions/week
- Ask for a simple proposal with their pricing and availability
- Do not over-commit until the pilot proves out

**Example format (from Willie McBride thread):**
> I want to keep this simple and start small. [Gap time slots] are our biggest opportunity. Let's start with a pilot — [X] mornings per week for [3–5] weeks. If it works and people show up, we expand from there.
>
> Can you send a simple proposal with your pricing and days that work?

### 6. Membership Questions

**Key facts:**
- **No minimum commitment** — single month is fine
- **Friend discount:** Members get the discount 4x/month (once per session opportunity)
- **Gift card → Membership payment:** System does NOT currently support this (Periode.no limitation, in progress). Workaround: manually suspend payment and apply gift card value; or issue credit toward next month.

**Template (gift card for membership):**
> Hi [Name] — still working out this functionality with our reservation system. Once it's resolved I'll apply your gift card balance to your membership. I'll follow up as soon as it's done.
> Davey

## FAQ — CUSTOMER-FACING

*These answers reflect current policy and can be used to update ebbandember.com/faq*

---

**Q: Where are you located?**
173 NE Bridgeton Rd, Portland, OR 97211. Parking is in the Ebb & Ember lot across from the east ramp. Follow posted signage and the map to the sauna boat.

---

**Q: How do I get my door code?**
Your door code appears in your booking portal once all waivers in your party are signed. Find it by clicking "Booking Info" in your confirmation email. Text 503-308-1293 if you need help on arrival.

---

**Q: What should I bring?**
- Swimsuit or comfortable sauna wear
- Two towels: one to sit on, one to dry off
- Full water bottle (avoid metal bottles — they get hot)
- Arrive well hydrated; avoid alcohol before your session

---

**Q: What's the difference between Social Sauna and Private?**
- **Social Sauna:** Shared experience — you book individual seats alongside other guests. Community-focused, phones-away vibe.
- **Private:** You book the entire boat for your group. Full privacy.

---

**Q: Can I cancel or reschedule?**
All sales are final. If you need to cancel, you can convert your booking to a gift card (store credit) for future use. Go to your confirmation email > "Booking Info" > "Cancel" or "Change Booking." Your gift card code will appear there.

For same-day illness or emergencies, email sauna@ebbandember.com or text 503-308-1293 and we'll issue you a gift card manually.

---

**Q: Can I use a gift card toward a membership?**
We're working on enabling this in our booking system — not available yet. Email us and we'll sort out a manual workaround.

---

**Q: Do memberships require a minimum commitment?**
No. You can purchase a single month at any tier. Cancel anytime.

---

**Q: How many times can a member bring a friend at a discount?**
Members can bring a friend at the member rate up to 4 times per month — once per session opportunity.

---

**Q: Is there a cold plunge?**
Yes — onboard rinse station, or you can take a dip in the river within the designated plunge zone.

---

**Q: Where is the bathroom?**
In the adjacent lounge area next to the floating sauna. Leave shoes in the lounge area before entering the sauna.

---

**Q: What's the age policy / other policies?**
See ebbandember.com/faq for the full rundown including weather, age policies, and on-site facilities.

---

**Q: Is the sauna boat docked or does it move?**
The sauna is permanently docked at our location. No captain onboard required — you access it directly from the dock.

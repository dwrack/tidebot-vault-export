## How to trigger

**Terminal:**
```
python3 ~/Scripts/ebb-ember/slot_post.py "Thursday 4pm"
```

**Remote trigger:** POST to your trigger URL with `{"slot": "Thursday 4pm"}`

**In Claude Code:** Just say `open thursday 4pm`

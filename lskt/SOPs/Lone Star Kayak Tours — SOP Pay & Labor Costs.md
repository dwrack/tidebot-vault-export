# Lone Star Kayak Tours — SOP: Pay & Labor Costs
*Last updated: 2026-04-07*

> **Purpose:** Establish clear pay rates across all roles so we can track labor costs accurately and avoid ad hoc compensation decisions.

---

## Guides (Houston)

Pay is per-trip and scales with group size. There is no hourly component.

| Group Size | Pay Per Trip |
|---|---|
| 2-3 guests (small tour) | $40 |
| Up to 8 guests (regular group) | $60 |
| 9+ guests (guide alone, solo-handling) | $80 |

---

## Rental Agents

Hourly pay. Most agents are at the base rate; a small number have been grandfathered to a higher rate.

| Tier | Rate |
|---|---|
| Base | $15/hour |
| Tenured (2-3+ years, raise approved) | $16.50/hour |

---

## Rental Staffing Logic

Rentals require on-site coverage but we don't staff speculatively. Current approach:

- **Mon-Wed (and sometimes Thurs):** No staff stationed if no reservations are on the books.
- **Busy or uncertain days:** Owner makes judgment calls on when to put someone on site vs. risk the no-show cost.
- **Slow days:** Staff is sent home after 3 hours minimum rather than keeping them on a dead shift.

The alternative is salaried coverage, which isn't viable at current revenue levels.

---

## Ad Hoc / Project Labor

When contract workers are brought in for one-off projects, pay is negotiated separately. Document all ad hoc labor with dates, hours, and project description for accounting purposes. Most recent reference rate: $15/hour for full-day site work.

# UGC Outreach & Repost Playbook — Lone Star Kayak Tours

**Owner:** Connor Champaign
**Frequency:** Weekly (15-20 min search + outreach as needed)

---

Same principle as Austin Kayak but with Lone Star's distinct brand. Customers are filming their tours. We need to find those videos and get them working for us.

## Outreach Templates

### Instagram DM

> Hey [name]! We saw your Reel from your kayak tour with us and it's awesome. Would you be cool with us reposting it on our page? We'd tag you and give full credit. Let us know!

### Facebook Comment

> Love this! Mind if we share it on our page? We'd tag you of course.

### Facebook Message

> Hey [name]! Loved the video from your tour with us. Would it be okay if we reposted it on our page? We'll tag you and give you a shoutout. Thanks!

### If They Say Yes

> Awesome, thank you! We'll tag you when it goes up. Hope to see you back out on the water soon!

### No Response After 3 Days (one follow-up max)

> Hey just circling back on this, totally no pressure either way!

---

## Story Recovery Outreach

Stories disappear after 24 hours but the person still has the video on their phone. When you see a great Story tagging or mentioning Lone Star Kayak, DM them right away and ask them to repost as a Reel or feed post so we can share it. We'll tag them in the repost and in any ad we run with it.

**Check story mentions/tags daily (2 min). Don't wait for the weekly search.**

### When You See a Great Story

> Hey [name]! Just saw your Story from your tour with us and it was great. Any chance you'd post it as a Reel or regular post? We'd love to share it on our page and tag you. We sometimes feature our favorite customer videos in ads too, and we'd tag you there as well. Totally up to you!

### If They Ask What They Get

> We'll tag you in everything: the repost on our feed and any ad we run with it. Free exposure to our whole audience. And honestly we just think your video was great and want more people to see it.

### If You Missed the Story (even weeks later)

> Hey [name]! You posted a Story from your kayak tour with us a little while back and it looked awesome. Any chance you still have the video and would be down to post it as a Reel? We'd love to share it and tag you, and we sometimes run our favorites as ads where you'd be tagged too.

## Repost Format

### Caption Template

> [Short caption matching the vibe]
>
> Thanks to @[creator_handle] for sharing their time on the water!
>
> #lonestarkayak #texaskayak #kayaktexas

**Rules:**
- Always tag the creator
- No sales pitch
- 2-3 lines max before the credit tag
- 3-5 hashtags

### Download & Save
- Use snapinsta.app (IG) or fdown.net (FB) to download
- Save to `Assets/Videos/UGC/` with filename: `2026-04-16_creator-handle_description.mp4`

### Posting Cadence
1-2 UGC reposts per week when content is available.

---

## Flag Top Performers for Ads

After a repost has been live 5-7 days, check performance. Flag it if:
- Views are 2x+ above your average
- Notable saves
- Comments asking about booking or pricing
- Shares significantly above average

**How to flag:** Message David in Slack with the post link, quick stats, and "recommend for ad." David handles all ad creation from UGC.

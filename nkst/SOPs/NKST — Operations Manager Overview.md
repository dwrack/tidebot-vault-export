# Nola Kayak Operations Manager

**New Orleans Kayak Swamp Tours**
Apres Assets LLC dba New Orleans Kayak Swamp Tours / Nola Kayak Tours

---

## Role Overview

The Operations Manager is the face of NKST in the community and the steady hand behind daily operations. This role focuses on building relationships, leading guides, and ensuring every tour runs smoothly. Routine administrative tasks are handled through automation, freeing the manager to focus on growth, presence, and the guest experience.

---

## Partnerships and Community

Develop and maintain the referral network that keeps tours full. Build relationships with hotels, concierges, event coordinators, and local hospitality professionals. Represent NKST at community events, mixers, and environmental programs. Cultivate corporate partnerships and coordinate special events, cleanups, and seasonal programming.

---

## Tour Scheduling and Optimization

Oversee the tour calendar to maximize efficiency and guest experience. Consolidate bookings, adjust availability based on demand and weather, and ensure every slot is staffed and ready. Coordinate location switches, minimum participation decisions, and any changes that affect guests or guides.

---

## Staff Management

Recruit, onboard, and support tour guides. Maintain compliance records, coordinate training, and foster a team culture guides want to be part of. Handle scheduling exceptions and escalate performance concerns to the owner.

---

## Customer Experience

Ensure guests have an exceptional experience from first contact to post-tour follow-up. Oversee inquiry responses, resolve issues, manage reviews, and close group bookings. Automation handles routine touchpoints -- the manager steps in where a human makes the difference.

---

## Marketing and Growth

Support social media presence, review platforms, and email campaigns. Contribute content, coordinate with guides on photo and storytelling, and support seasonal promotions. Identify opportunities for new offerings and bring them to the owner.

---

## Systems and Operations

Oversee the booking platform, CRM, and automation tools. Review daily briefings, catch workflow failures, and keep all systems accurate. Maintain organized documentation and secure access to company accounts.

---

## Fleet and Equipment

Oversee vehicle readiness, equipment inventory, and maintenance scheduling. Review the weekly vehicle accountability log and coordinate any repairs or supply needs with the owner.

---

## Compliance and Permits

Keep guide credentials current -- CPR certifications, Louisiana guiding permits, and contractor agreements. Prepare and submit quarterly LDWF reports. File incident reports promptly and keep the owner informed.

---

## Weekly Responsibilities

Review the operations summary. Audit upcoming bookings for accuracy. Respond to outstanding reviews. Check guide compliance. Adjust availability for the next four weeks. Follow up on open partnership leads.

---

## Monthly Responsibilities

Review guide field notes and schedule coaching conversations. Evaluate active partnerships and re-engage where needed. Review automation performance and marketing results. Submit any quarterly reports due. Send the owner a brief ops summary.

---

## What Automation Handles

Booking confirmations, pre-tour guest sequences, review requests, guide manifests, missed call responses, FAQ chatbot, compliance reminders, payroll calculations, and weekly reporting summaries are all handled by GoHighLevel and Claude. The manager reviews outputs, acts on exceptions, and focuses time on the work that requires a person.

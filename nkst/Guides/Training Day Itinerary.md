## Day Of

**Arrive at 1407 Piety Street at 9:00 AM**

Bring: Water, Driver's License

---

## Morning — Logistics & Launch

**9:30 AM — Depart for LaPlace, LA**

At the gas station:
- Pick up the trailer
- Demonstrate: how to connect the trailer, what to look for to confirm it's secured, and safe driving with a trailer

At the launch site:
- Unloading trailer safely — how to unload without injury, and where to ask for help
- Where kayaks are staged
- How to convert kayaks from singles to tandems
- Where life jackets and paddles live
- How to fit life jackets properly
- Paddle lesson

---

## On-Water Training

**Frontloading with guests:**
- Alligators — what to expect
- Paddle signals
- When and why we group up
- Invite questions — *this is where customer service skills shine*
  - Make guests feel comfortable asking about history, ecology, wildlife
  - If you don't know the answer: *"I'm not sure, but I know ___ — I'll get back to you with a better answer. Is that okay?"*

**Kayak ratios:**
- Most guests go in tandems; some singles depending on group size
- 10 kayaks per trailer maximum
- 12:1 client-to-guide ratio

---

## Post-Tour

- Load kayaks back onto trailer (all facing same direction), secure straps
- Return to Piety Street base
- PFD care per [[Operations/PFD Washing Procedure]]
- Lock everything

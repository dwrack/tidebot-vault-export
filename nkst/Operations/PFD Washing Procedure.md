# PFD Washing Procedure

Source: Google Drive import — March 2026

---

## Supplies Needed
- Two washtubs
- Dawn dish soap
- Mirazyme odor eliminator
- Scrub brush
- Hose
- Hangers
- Dehumidifier (in shipping container)

---

## Steps

1. **Set up tubs** in a sunny area near the hose
   - Tub 1: Dawn dish soap (one generous squirt) + water
   - Tub 2: 2 capfuls of Mirazyme + water
   - **Tip:** Fill tubs before the tour so water is warm and ready when you return

2. **Bring out PFDs and hangers**

3. **Wash**
   - Dunk PFD into soapy water (Tub 1)
   - Use scrub brush on muddy spots and stains

4. **Rinse**
   - Dunk PFD into Mirazyme water (Tub 2) — 3 to 4 times

5. **Hang outside to dry** until no longer dripping

6. **Move into shipping container** to finish drying
   - Leave **3–4 inches of space** between wet PFDs
   - **Make sure the dehumidifier is ON**

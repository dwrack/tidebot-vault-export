## Common Van Reminders

- Gas cards **only work at Shell stations**
- Check straps and trailer hookups before every departure
- **Double check: TRAILER WHEELS UNLOCKED before driving away**
- Return van to 1407 Piety St — vacuum/hose down as needed
- Ensure sufficient gas for next tour after each use
- Lock car, container, and gate when leaving Piety St

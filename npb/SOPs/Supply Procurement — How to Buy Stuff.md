# Supply Procurement — How to Buy Stuff

*For JT and marina staff. Effective April 2026.*

---

## The Problem We're Fixing

Supplies run out, JT calls Jeffrey, Jeffrey calls someone, nothing happens for a week. Meanwhile the marina looks rough and work stalls. This process fixes that.

---

## JT's Company Card

JT has a company credit card with a **$500/month cap**.

### Spending Tiers

**Under $100 - Just buy it.**
- Buy what you need
- Text a photo of the receipt to Jeffrey
- Done

**$100 to $500 - Text Jeffrey first.**
- Text Jeffrey what you need and why
- Wait for approval (should be same day)
- Buy it, text receipt photo

**Over $500 - Needs Jeffrey + ownership approval.**
- Text Jeffrey and David
- Wait for both to approve
- Buy it, text receipt photo

---

## Weekly Supply Order

For non-urgent, recurring supplies (cleaning products, trash bags, paper towels, boat supplies):

| Day | What Happens |
|-----|-------------|
| **Sunday** | JT submits supply list to Jeffrey via Slack or text |
| **Monday** | Jeffrey or David places Amazon order |
| **Wednesday** | Supplies arrive at marina |

If something is urgent and can't wait until the next cycle, use the spending tiers above and buy it yourself.

---

## What Gets Ordered Regularly

This list will grow as we track what we're actually using. Start here:

**Cleaning**
- Industrial trash bags (55 gallon)
- Multi-surface cleaner (gallon jugs)
- Bleach
- Paper towels (bulk)
- Disposable gloves
- Toilet paper
- Spray bottles

**Boat Supplies**
- Garbage bags for boats
- Soap for restrooms
- Paper towels for restrooms

**Grounds**
- Weed eater string/line
- Blower fuel (2-cycle mix if needed)
- Landscaping supplies as needed

---

## Tracking

Over time, we'll build a recurring order template based on what actually gets used. JT: keep a running note on your phone of what runs out and when. After a month or two, we'll have a pattern and can set up auto-reorders or at least a predictable list.

---

## Rules

- Never let critical supplies run out completely. If you're on the last roll of toilet paper, that's an emergency buy, not a Sunday list item.
- Keep receipts. Photo to Jeffrey every time.
- If the card gets declined or you hit the cap, text David immediately.
- No personal purchases on the company card. This should be obvious.

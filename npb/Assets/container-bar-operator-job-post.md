## Post Title

Bar Manager / Operator - Waterfront Container Bar (Jean Lafitte / Barataria)

## Post Body

We're building a shipping container bar at a waterfront marina south of New Orleans, and we're looking for someone to run it.

This isn't a corporate gig. It's a ground-floor opportunity to build something from scratch at a marina that already gets 1,000+ visitors on weekends for our party boat tours. The bar is the next piece of a bigger vision to turn this property into a waterfront destination.

**What the job is:**

You'd manage the container bar from buildout to daily operations. That means helping source equipment, setting up the bar, hiring 1-2 bartenders, managing inventory, and running service on weekends (expanding to weekdays as demand grows).

The marina is on the water in the Jean Lafitte / Barataria area, about 30 minutes south of downtown New Orleans. It's not a fancy spot right now. It's a working marina with party boats, docks, and bayou. But the location is unbeatable and there's nothing like it in the region for people who want a waterfront hangout.

**What we're looking for:**

- Bar or hospitality management experience (2+ years)
- Comfortable working outdoors in a marina environment
- Can manage inventory, staff, and cash independently
- Knows how to create a vibe, not just pour drinks
- Reliable, self-directed, doesn't need hand-holding
- Louisiana bartending experience is a plus but not required
- Bonus: experience with bar buildouts, container conversions, or pop-up bar concepts

**Compensation:**

Open to discussion. Options include revenue share, flat monthly lease + percentage, or salaried management. Depends on your experience and how much ownership you want to take.

**Hours:**

Starting with Friday through Sunday, aligned with tour schedules. Expanding to 7 days based on demand. Flexible for the right person.

**About us:**

NOLA Party Barge runs party boat tours out of a marina in Barataria. We've been operating for several years and we're growing. The container bar is the first step in a bigger plan to make the marina a place people come to hang out, eat, drink, and enjoy the waterfront, even without getting on a boat.

**To apply:**

Send a short email to [EMAIL] with:
- Your name and phone number
- A few sentences about your bar/hospitality experience
- What interests you about this kind of gig

No resume required, but send one if you have it. We're looking for the right person, not the right piece of paper.

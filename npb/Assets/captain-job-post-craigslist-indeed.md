# Head Captain - Nola Party Barge (New Orleans, LA)

**Full-Time | Competitive Pay | Year-Round**

Nola Party Barge runs Coast Guard-inspected party boats and eco-tour vessels on the waterways of New Orleans. We're looking for an experienced Head Captain to lead our on-water crew and keep operations running safe and smooth.

This isn't a desk job. You'll be a working captain running trips, managing deckhands, and keeping our fleet in compliance and good shape. If you like being on the water, working with people, and running a tight ship (literally), this is the gig.

## What You'll Do

- Operate USCG-inspected passenger vessels daily
- Train and supervise deckhands and junior captains
- Run pre-departure safety briefings and enforce emergency protocols
- Complete daily safety, ops, and maintenance checklists
- Prep for and assist with annual Coast Guard inspections
- Conduct and document required drills and safety trainings
- Perform routine vessel inspections and basic maintenance
- Work with 4-stroke outboard motors (troubleshooting, routine service)
- Pull, launch, and secure vessels on trailers as needed
- Keep the vessel maintenance logbook current

## What You Need

- **USCG 100-Ton Master Captain's License** (current)
- **Valid TWIC card**
- **First Aid / CPR certification** (current)
- Must meet all USCG medical and drug-testing requirements
- Experience operating inspected passenger vessels
- Basic mechanical aptitude with marine engines
- Calm under pressure, good with guests, strong communicator

## What You Get

- Leadership role in a growing marine tourism company
- Competitive pay based on experience and credentials
- Consistent schedule, year-round operations
- Real input into how we run things and build our safety culture

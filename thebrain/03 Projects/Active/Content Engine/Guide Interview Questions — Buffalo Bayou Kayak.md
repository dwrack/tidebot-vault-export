# Guide Interview Questions — Buffalo Bayou Kayak

*For: Connor Champaign + team*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per guide*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
Urban nature, the surprise of wildlife in the middle of Houston, and the "your backyard is wild" realization.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "What's the most surprising animal you've seen on the bayou, and what did the guests do?"
2. "People think of Buffalo Bayou as a drainage ditch. What do they say when they're actually on it?"
3. "Describe a stretch of the bayou where you forget you're in Houston."
4. "What's the difference between this paddle and a kayak trip somewhere 'scenic' like Hill Country?"
5. "Has a guest ever brought their kid and had one of those 'this is why we live here' moments?"
6. "What does the bayou look like from water level that you can't see from the trail?"
7. "Is there a time of year when the bayou feels completely different?"
8. "What do you know about this waterway that most Houstonians don't?"
9. "A guest says 'I wasn't sure this would be worth it.' What usually changes their mind?"
10. "If you could put a camera anywhere on the bayou for one hour, where would it be and when?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- Wildlife (any kind, the more surprising the better given it's urban)
- The stretch of bayou that feels least like Houston
- A guest reacting to something they didn't expect to see

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

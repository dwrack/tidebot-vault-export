# Guide Interview Questions — Ebb & Ember (Portland Floating Sauna)

*For: Jonah Moses, and any staff who interact with guests*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per person*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
The cold plunge moment, the simplicity of heat and cold, and the anti-performative-wellness honesty.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "Describe the face someone makes the second after the cold plunge. Not the photo version, the real version."
2. "What's the difference between someone arriving and someone leaving? Physically, their energy, how they talk."
3. "Portland has a million wellness things. What makes a floating sauna on the river different from all of it?"
4. "Has anyone ever come in stressed or upset and left different? What happened?"
5. "What's the most honest thing a guest has said about the experience? Not the polished review, the real reaction."
6. "You're on the water. What does that add? Could this be a sauna in a building and feel the same?"
7. "What do you hear from the sauna? Describe the sounds."
8. "Is there a time of day or a season when the experience hits completely differently?"
9. "A guest says 'I'm not really a sauna person.' What happens next?"
10. "What's the thing about this experience that you can't explain to someone, they just have to feel it?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- Steam rising off the sauna with the river in the background
- Someone's reaction right after the cold plunge (with permission)
- The view from the sauna at the best time of day

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

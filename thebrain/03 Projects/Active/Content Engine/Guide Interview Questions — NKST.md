# Guide Interview Questions — NKST (Swamp Kayak)

*For: Dylan LP, Michelle Healey, JT Sides, Connor (bat tours), any active guides*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per guide*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
Silence, wildlife, and the feeling that you're seeing something real that most tourists never find.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "Take me through the moment you enter the bayou. What changes? The sound, the light, the air."
2. "What's the closest you've ever been to a gator without the guests noticing first?"
3. "There's a moment on most tours where everyone just goes quiet. What triggers that?"
4. "What do you know about this swamp that a motorboat guide will never understand?"
5. "A guest once told you something after a tour that stuck with you. What was it?"
6. "If someone said 'I already did a swamp tour on a motorboat, why would I do yours?' what would you say?"
7. "What does this bayou smell like at 7am? Describe it like I've never been outside."
8. "What's the most unexpected wildlife moment you've had out there?"
9. "Is there a regular guest or a local character you see out on the water?"
10. "What's the thing about New Orleans that only people who work on the water know?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- Sunrise/early morning setup
- Their favorite spot on the water
- The moment guests arrive

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

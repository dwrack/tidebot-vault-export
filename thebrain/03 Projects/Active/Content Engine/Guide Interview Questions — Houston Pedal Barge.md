# Guide Interview Questions — Houston Pedal Barge

*For: Connor Champaign, Bella (lead captain), captains*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per person*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
Houston city pride, the skyline from the water, and proving that Houston has more personality than people think.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "What's the reaction when people see the Houston skyline from the water for the first time?"
2. "You hear 'there's nothing to do in Houston' all the time. What happens on this barge that proves that wrong?"
3. "Describe the energy of a corporate group vs. a birthday group vs. a bachelorette. What's different?"
4. "What's the moment on a trip where you know this group is going to remember this?"
5. "Has a Houstonian ever told you they've lived here for years and never knew this existed? What happened?"
6. "What does Houston look like from the water at sunset?"
7. "What's the funniest thing that's ever happened on the barge?"
8. "A guest is on the fence about booking for their group. What do you tell them?"
9. "What's different about captaining a pedal barge vs. any other job on the water?"
10. "If Houston had a personality, what would it be? And how does the barge capture that?"

### For Bella specifically (lead captain)
"What's it like being the lead captain? What do you do to set the tone before a group even gets on?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- The Houston skyline from the water at golden hour
- A group mid-trip when the energy is highest
- The barge before anyone's on it, just the setup

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec captain/crew bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

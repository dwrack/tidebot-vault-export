# Guide Interview Questions — Austin Kayak Tours / LSKT

*For: Connor Champaign + team*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per guide*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
The surprise that this exists 15 minutes from downtown, and the contrast between "Austin the brand" and Austin the actual landscape.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "Someone paddles for the first time and looks around. What do they usually say?"
2. "What's the most common reaction from locals who live in Austin but have never been on the river?"
3. "Describe what the river looks like at the time of day you like best."
4. "What wildlife shows up that blows people's minds, given that we're in the middle of a city?"
5. "If you had to describe the difference between being on 6th Street and being on the river, in one sentence, what would you say?"
6. "Has a guest ever said something about Austin after a tour that changed how you see the city?"
7. "What's the stretch of water you look forward to every single time?"
8. "Someone's visiting Austin for 48 hours. They can do one thing. Make the case for the kayak."
9. "What do people not realize about paddling until they're actually doing it?"
10. "Is there a regular, a local who keeps coming back? What draws them?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- The river at their favorite time of day
- Wildlife they spot (even just a turtle counts)
- The look on someone's face when they realize how close this is to downtown

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

# Guide Interview Questions — DCKT (Door County Kayak Tours)

*For: Lea, Jackson, returning seasonal guides*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per guide*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
The sea cave reveal, the warmth of the guides, and the feeling of being let in on a Door County secret.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "Walk me through the sea cave approach. What do you do right before the group enters? How do you set that moment up?"
2. "What's the most emotional reaction you've gotten from a guest inside the caves?"
3. "A first-timer shows up nervous about kayaking. What do you do in the first 5 minutes to change that?"
4. "What's something about Door County that even repeat visitors don't know?"
5. "Is there a moment on the tour where you watch people stop being tourists and start just... being present?"
6. "What's the view from the water that you never get tired of?"
7. "Tell me about a kid on a tour who had a moment. Something clicked for them."
8. "What do you notice about families on the water that's different from families on land?"
9. "You've paddled this shoreline hundreds of times. What do you still notice?"
10. "If someone said 'we already did a kayak tour with [competitor],' what would you say is different about ours?"

### For Lea specifically (she does social)
"What content have you posted that got way more engagement than you expected? What was it about?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- The sea cave approach from the water
- Their favorite view from the shoreline
- A candid moment with guests (with permission)

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

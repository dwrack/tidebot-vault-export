# Guide Interview Questions — NOLA Party Barge

*For: Jeffrey Chitek, JT Sides, captains, musicians*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per person*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
The first note of live music on the water, the skyline, and the moment a group goes from "this is fun" to "this is the best night of the trip."

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "Describe the moment the music starts and the boat pulls away from the dock. What happens to the group?"
2. "What's the wildest bachelorette party moment you've seen on this barge?"
3. "There's a point in every trip where the energy shifts. Something clicks. What is that moment?"
4. "What does New Orleans look like from the water that you can't see from land?"
5. "Has a guest ever told you this was the best part of their trip? What happened on that trip?"
6. "What's the difference between a good night on the barge and a great one?"
7. "You've seen Bourbon Street and you've seen this. What's the real New Orleans?"
8. "What do you see on guests' faces when the sunset hits during a trip?"
9. "Tell me about a group that almost didn't book, or showed up skeptical, and what happened."
10. "What's a song or a moment from a musician on the barge that gave you chills?"

### For the musicians specifically
"What's it like playing live on the water vs. in a bar? What changes?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- The dock before guests arrive
- The moment the music starts
- A reaction shot from the group mid-trip

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide/captain/musician bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

# Guide Interview Questions — Door County Ebike Tours

*For: Ebike guides (coordinate with Bella)*
*Format: 15-20 min video call per guide*
*Purpose: Bio footage + content source material*

## The magic here
The landscape from a different speed, covering more ground than a hike but slower than a car, and seeing Door County's roads, orchards, and shoreline the way locals do.

## Open (every call)
"Tell me your name, what you do, and how you ended up doing this."

## Questions

1. "What's the stretch of road or trail that makes people pull over and just stare?"
2. "Someone's done the kayak tour in the morning. Now they're on the ebike. What's different about how they see Door County?"
3. "What do you pass on the ebike that people in cars blow right by?"
4. "Describe the feeling of the ebike on a downhill with the lake in view."
5. "What's the thing about Door County from the road that surprises visitors?"
6. "Has anyone ever said the ebike tour was better than the kayak tour? What did they mean?"
7. "What's your favorite stop, not the obvious one, the one only you know about?"
8. "If you had 30 seconds to convince someone to do the ebike instead of renting a car, what would you say?"

## Close (every call)
"What do you want people to know about you that has nothing to do with this job?"

## After the call
Ask them to film 3 things on their phone next shift:
- The best view from the ebike route
- Something you pass that cars miss
- A guest's reaction to a downhill or a vista

## What we're cutting from this
- 60-90 sec guide bio clip for website
- 15-30 sec social cuts for IG/TikTok
- Pull quotes for booking confirmation emails

# UGC Outreach & Repost Playbook — Master Template

*Central reference copy. Business-specific versions live in each vault's Marketing/ folder.*

---

## Why This Matters

UGC video outperforms brand-produced content on every metric that matters: trust, engagement, click-through, cost-per-acquisition. Customers are already filming their experiences with us. We just need to find those videos, get permission, repost them, and turn the best ones into ads.

This is low-effort, high-return content. No production costs, no scheduling shoots. Just monitoring, outreach, and reposting.

---

## Step 1: Find UGC Videos

Check these sources weekly (15-20 min per business):

### Instagram
- **Location tag**: Search the business location on IG, filter to Reels
- **Hashtags**: Search each business hashtag (see business-specific list)
- **Tagged posts**: Check tagged photos/videos on the business profile
- **Mentions**: Check notifications for story mentions and tags

### Facebook
- **Tagged posts**: Check the business page's "Posts about [Business]" section
- **Group mentions**: Search relevant local tourism/activity groups
- **Check-ins**: Review recent check-ins on the business page
- **Reviews with media**: Scan recent reviews for attached videos

### TikTok
- Search business name and location-specific hashtags
- Check if anyone has tagged the business location

## Step 2: Reach Out for Permission

**Do this the same day you find the video.** Speed matters because creators are most excited about their content right after posting.

### Instagram DM Template

> Hey [name]! We saw your [Reel/video] from your [tour/experience] with us and it's amazing. Would you be cool with us reposting it on our page? We'd tag you and give full credit. Let us know!

### Facebook Comment Template
*(Comment on their post first, then follow up via message if they respond positively)*

> This is so good! Mind if we share this on our page? We'd tag you of course.

### Facebook Message Template

> Hey [name]! Loved the video you posted from your [experience] with us. Would it be okay if we reposted it on our page? We'll tag you and give you a shoutout. Thanks!

### If They Say Yes

Reply with something like:
> Awesome, thank you! We'll tag you when it goes up. Hope to see you back out here soon!

### If No Response After 3 Days
One follow-up is fine. After that, move on.

> Hey just circling back on this, totally no pressure either way!

## Step 2B: Story Recovery Outreach

Stories vanish after 24 hours, but the person still has the video on their phone. If you see a great Story mentioning or tagging the business, DM them right away and ask them to repost it as a Reel or feed post so we can share it.

**Why this works:** People are flattered you noticed. Most will repost if you make the ask easy and give them a reason (we'll tag you, you'll get exposure on our page, and we may feature you in a paid ad where you'll be tagged too).

### When You See a Great Story — DM Immediately

> Hey [name]! Just saw your Story from [the tour / the barge / the sauna] and it was so good. Any chance you'd post it as a Reel or regular post? We'd love to share it on our page and tag you. We sometimes feature our favorite customer videos in ads too, and we'd tag you there as well. Totally up to you!

### If They Ask "What Do I Get?"

> We'll tag you in everything: the repost on our feed and any ad we run with it. Free exposure to our whole audience. And honestly we just think your video was great and want more people to see it.

### If They Post It

> You're the best, thank you! We'll tag you when we share it. Keep an eye out!

### Key Timing Note
You have to catch Stories within 24 hours. Build the habit of checking story mentions/tags daily, not just weekly. Takes 2 minutes. If you miss a great Story and it's gone, you can still DM and ask if they'd be willing to post it fresh:

> Hey [name]! You posted a Story from your [experience] with us a little while back and it was incredible. Any chance you still have the video and would be down to post it as a Reel? We'd love to share it and tag you, and we sometimes run our favorites as ads where you'd be tagged too.

This works surprisingly well even weeks later. People keep good videos on their phone.

## Step 3: Download & Repost

### How to Download
- **Instagram Reels**: Use a Reel downloader (snapinsta.app or similar). Save to the business Assets/Videos/UGC/ folder.
- **Facebook Videos**: Use a FB video downloader (fdown.net or similar). Same save location.
- **TikTok**: Download from TikTok directly (save video option) or use snaptik.app.

### Caption Format for Reposts

> [Short caption that matches the vibe of the original — keep it natural, not salesy]
>
> Thanks to @[creator_handle] for sharing their experience with us!
>
> #[business hashtag] #[location hashtag] #[activity hashtag]

**Rules:**
- Always tag the creator
- Don't add a sales pitch. The video IS the pitch.
- Keep captions short (2-3 lines max before the tag)
- Use 3-5 hashtags, not 30

### Posting Cadence
Aim for 1-2 UGC reposts per week when you have enough content. Don't force it if the video quality isn't there.

## Step 4: Flag Top Performers for Ads

After a UGC repost has been live for 5-7 days, check its performance:

### Flag It If:
- **Views**: 2x or more above your average Reel/video views
- **Saves**: Any video with notable saves (people saving = high purchase intent)
- **Comments**: Genuine comments asking about booking, pricing, or "where is this?"
- **Shares**: Significantly above average

### How to Flag
Add a note to the business Slack channel (or text David directly) with:
- Link to the post
- Quick stats (views, saves, comments)
- "Recommend for ad" or "strong performer"

David will review flagged videos and decide which ones become paid ads. Don't run ads from these yourself.

## Step 5: Organize & Track

### Folder Structure
Create an `Assets/Videos/UGC/` folder in the business vault if one doesn't exist. Save downloaded videos there with filenames like:
`2026-04-16_creator-handle_description.mp4`

### Simple Tracking
Keep a running list at the bottom of this doc (or a separate note) with:
- Date found
- Creator handle
- Platform (IG/FB/TT)
- Permission status (asked / approved / declined / no response)
- Reposted? (yes/no + date)
- Flagged for ad? (yes/no)
